Description

“RowRow” is a unique sliding puzzle game with more than 80 level of hand crafted stages and diverse puzzle logics. It is as simple as matching shapes. Align rows of shapes, sum them up and match the border-lined shapes to clear a stage.

Feature

Minimalistic Design

The minimal design of the shapes and carefully selected colors will give players more concentration and relaxing, soothing experience.

Whole New Puzzle

Completely new type of puzzle game, mixing the sliding factor and arithmetic calculation with shapes.

Simple Shapes, Intuitive Recognition

No need to memorize difficult logics to play. Each point of a shape is the number assigned. Just match number with the empty shape.

Simple Operation

You can add shapes in other blocks. No need to subtract, multiply, or divide. You do not need to take notes and write them down.

Blocking Block

You can always use the blocking blocks to set the addition area. Blocks behind blocking block is not summed up.

Coming Soon

Our Wayway Team is working on 2 more games on the ‘Shape Series’. If you liked our game ‘RowRow’ please look forward to our next games.

History

Wayway, we are an indie game development team came together in 2018. There are 3 team members who each specializes in different areas. We think that game as a new media has a great potential to not only give people an enjoyable time but convey meaning and messages to all who participate.
Starting with RowRow(2019) we are developing several games. Minimal puzzle games with the shape concept, an adventure game that actively engage with light and sound, a survival game inspired by the beauty of animals.
